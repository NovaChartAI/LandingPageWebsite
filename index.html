About NovaChart

At NovaChart, we believe every minute spent by a healthcare specialist is invaluable. That's why we're dedicated to transforming the way clinical documentation is handled. Too often, skilled professionals are bogged down by administrative tasks, diverting precious time and energy away from direct patient interaction and care.

Our mission is to restore that balance. By leveraging cutting-edge AI, NovaChart empowers clinicians with intelligent tools that seamlessly record patient-doctor interactions and instantly generate accurate, comprehensive clinical notes. This not only streamlines workflows and reduces the burden of paperwork but also helps combat burnout, allowing healthcare providers to focus on what they do best: delivering exceptional patient care.

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of privacy, security, and accuracy, ensuring our solution integrates smoothly and reliably into the demanding healthcare environment. NovaChart is more than just an app; it's a partner in enhancing the quality and efficiency of healthcare for everyone.
